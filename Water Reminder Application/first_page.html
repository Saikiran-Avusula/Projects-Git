Water Reminder Application.

Working Principle


When you enter your credentials in the form, our Water Reminder Application takes your inputs and validates your water consumption based on your goal and awake time.

Here's how it works:
You enter your name, awake time, and water intake goal. Our app calculates how much water you should drink each hour to reach your goal.

Let's say you're Sai Kiran, awake for 8 hours, with a 4-liter water intake goal. Our app reminds you to drink 1 liter of water four times per an hour, helping you stay hydrated and reach your goal.
